Designed for real PCB inspection

  • Multiple file formats.
    Gerber RS-274X/X2 (inner layers included), Excellon drill files, ODB++ jobs (panels expanded), IPC-2581 designs, Eagle boards, Gerber job files (.gbrjob), IPC-D-356 netlists and CSV/TXT/POS component placements.
  • Layer control.
    Show, hide, reorder, lock, recolor, duplicate and export layers from a touch-friendly panel.
  • Editing tools.
    Move, duplicate, resize and delete shapes, including rounded rectangles and editable corner radius where supported.
  • Transform workflows.
    Mirror, rotate, reset and panelize layers, or apply transformations to a multi-selection group.
  • Measurement tools.
    Measure center distance, dx/dy, angle, midpoint, clearance and overlap, then export measurements as CSV.
  • Component search.
    Import component files with parser profiles, search by reference, value or package, and highlight top or bottom components.
  • Net search.
    Nets from IPC-D-356 netlists, ODB++ jobs or IPC-2581 files: search any net and highlight every one of its pads — or tap a shape to reveal its net.
  • Fast workflow.
    Recent files on a long-press of Open, drag & drop from the Files app, and board statistics (size, hole table, smallest drill and trace).

Local by design

Your PCB files are opened and processed on your device. AGBoard does not track users and does not sell personal data.

Field friendly

Use pinch zoom, pan, layer filters, realistic colors, grid and snap settings to inspect boards anywhere.

Export ready

Create HD PNG images, multi-page vector PDFs (one page per layer), layer CSV reports, light DRC CSV reports, AirPrint output and shareable project files.

How it works

Open

On both iPhone and iPad, open files from the Files app or iCloud — a single file or a whole fabrication archive, every layer at once. On iPad you can also drag & drop them straight onto the canvas.

Inspect

Zoom to the smallest pad, toggle layers, measure distances and clearances, and find any component or net in seconds.

Export

Share HD PNG images, multi-page vector PDFs, CSV reports — or a project file your team can reopen as-is.

Frequently asked questions

Which file formats are supported?

Gerber RS-274X/X2 (inner layers included), Excellon drill files, ODB++ jobs with panels expanded, IPC-2581 designs, Eagle .brd boards, Gerber job files (.gbrjob), IPC-D-356 netlists and CSV/TXT/POS component placements.

Are my design files private?

Yes. Every file is parsed and rendered on your device — nothing is uploaded, no account is required and the app contains no tracking.

Is the app free?

AGBoard is free to download. Start a monthly or annual subscription to use the 3-day free trial; cancel during the trial to avoid being charged. If you do not cancel, the selected plan renews automatically through your Apple Account.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later. Rendering is GPU-accelerated (Metal), so even dense multi-layer boards stay smooth.

Is a Mac or Windows version planned?

Yes — Mac and Windows versions are planned for December 2026.
