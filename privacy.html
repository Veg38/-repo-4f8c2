Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 June 2026

AGBoard - PCBEditor ("the app") is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, track, sell or share your personal data. This policy explains what that means in practice.

Data we collect

None. The app does not collect any personal data, and it contains no analytics, advertising or third-party tracking SDKs.

Your PCB files

Gerber, Excellon, ODB++, Eagle, netlist and component placement files that you open are read and processed locally on your iPhone or iPad. They are never uploaded to us or to any third party. The app has no server component and does not require an account or an internet connection to view your boards.

Settings stored on your device

The app stores your preferences (such as display options, saved component parser profiles and recent files) locally on your device using the standard iOS settings store. This information stays on your device and is included in your own device backups if you have them enabled. It is not transmitted to us.

Photo library

If you choose to export a board image, the app asks permission to save that image to your photo library. The app only writes the image you explicitly export; it does not read or browse your existing photos.

Sharing and exports

When you export a PNG, PDF, CSV report or project file, that file is created on your device and shared only through the destinations you choose via the standard iOS share sheet. Those destinations (for example Mail or Files) are governed by their own privacy policies.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is rated 4+.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@agboard-app.com.
